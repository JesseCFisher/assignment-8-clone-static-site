Family-run, farm-fresh,
hand-cut quality

In 1959, Jack Gartner opened a small meat market with a simple business plan - make people happy and they'll keep coming back. Today, Gartner's Country Meat Market is Portland's favorite family-run source for fresh-cut steaks, chops, ribs and roasts, handcrafted sausages, ham, bacon and a whole lot more. We sell happiness by the pound - and we never forget what Jack taught us: People come for what's inside the case. They come back for the service behind the counter. See our FAQ page.

Custom cutting and processing

Great butcher shops provide the “extras” that make their shopping experience stand apart from the rest. That’s how Gartner’s has done business for nearly 60 years, and we’re proud to offer Custom Cutting and Game Processing as special services to our customers every day.
